MinCal – Nutrition & Weight Tracker
A thoughtfully designed health tracker that empowers you to build long-term, sustainable wellness habits with AI-enhanced insights and intuitive visual feedback.

Track What Matters Most
MinCal makes it easy to log your daily nutrition and weight progress through a sleek, minimalist interface. With a focus on simplicity and clarity, users can quickly record meals, monitor calories, and see their wellness progress over time using dynamic visualizations.

AI-Powered Calorie Estimation
Leveraging OpenAI's advanced APIs, MinCal helps estimate calorie counts with smart AI prompts, reducing the guesswork and making food logging faster and more accurate—even from photo inputs or simple text descriptions.

Privacy-Focused & Cloud Synced
Your data is securely stored and synced in the cloud using a robust backend powered by AWS EC2 and DynamoDB. Authentication is handled via AWS Cognito, ensuring complete privacy and seamless access across devices.
